JOIN the fight against internet censorship

Help us collect censorship data from your country

Please download our app. When you run it on your computer, it automatically goes through a list of 4000 web-sites and tells us if they are blocked in your country. The app is very small and is completely safe to run. It will not interfere with your normal computer activity

No installation is needed. All you need to do is to download and run this app on your computer.

You can run this app more than once on different days to provide even more accurate data. But running it even once helps us a lot.

This app works on Windows only.
